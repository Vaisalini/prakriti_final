DIET
For Vata, focus on grounding, nourishing foods such as root vegetables, healthy fats, and warm meals. Include warming spices like ginger, cinnamon, and turmeric to enhance digestion.
VATTA-PITTA
For Vata-Pitta balance, choose warm, mildly spiced foods, stay hydrated, avoid overstimulation, and maintain a calm daily routine with regular meals.
EXERCISES
For Vata, gentle yoga or stretching routines like slow-flow vinyasa or yin yoga help calm the mind and body. Keep the exercises steady and avoid overexertion.
