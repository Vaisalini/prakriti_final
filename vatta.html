DIET
A Vata-balancing diet includes warm, moist, and grounding foods like cooked grains, root vegetables, ghee, nuts, and soothing herbal teas.
VATTA
Vata is one of the three doshas in Ayurveda, representing air and space. It governs movement, communication, and the nervous system.
EXCERCISES
Vata types benefit from grounding, calming exercises like yoga, walking, tai chi, or swimming—done in a slow, steady, and routine manner.
