DIET
For Vatta-Kapha, focus on light, warm, and easily digestible foods. Include grains like quinoa and barley, and avoid heavy, oily foods. Astringent flavors like leafy greens and bitter vegetables are beneficial to balance excess Kapha.
VATTA-KAPHA
For Vatta-Kapha balance, eat warm and spiced foods to stimulate digestion. Choose a mix of grounding foods for Vatta and light foods for Kapha. Avoid dairy and cold foods that can aggravate Kapha.
EXERCISES
For Vatta-Kapha, moderate intensity exercises like brisk walking, jogging, or cycling can help balance both energies. Incorporate strengthening routines like Pilates or yoga with some cardio to stimulate circulation.
