DIET
For Pitta, focus on cooling, light, and hydrating foods. Favor fruits, vegetables, and grains that are sweet, bitter, and astringent, such as cucumbers, melons, leafy greens, and barley. Avoid spicy, acidic, and greasy foods.
PITTA
To balance Pitta, incorporate foods that are cooling and calming. Eat plenty of sweet and bitter foods, such as coconut, dairy, and leafy greens. Minimize the intake of hot, spicy, and fried foods.
EXERCISES
For Pitta, choose calming exercises like swimming, yoga, or walking in nature. Avoid overly intense or competitive activities that may raise heat and stress. Focus on relaxation and balance.
