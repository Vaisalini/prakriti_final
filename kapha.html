DIET
For Kapha, focus on light, warm, and dry foods. Avoid heavy, oily, and sweet foods. Choose spicy, bitter, and astringent foods that will help balance Kapha's natural heaviness. Include plenty of vegetables, legumes, and whole grains, and reduce dairy, fried foods, and sugar intake.
KAPHA
For Kapha, aim for a diet that stimulates and reduces excess weight. Foods with bitter, pungent, and astringent tastes are ideal. Minimize dairy, fats, and sweet foods while emphasizing light, fresh, and low-fat meals to reduce Kapha's naturally heavy, cool, and moist qualities.
EXERCISES
Kapha individuals benefit from vigorous exercises to stimulate circulation and increase energy levels. Engage in activities like jogging, cycling, hiking, and aerobic exercises. Aim for regular, challenging workouts to balance Kapha’s tendency toward sluggishness.
